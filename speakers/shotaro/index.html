Elastic Technical Product Marketing Manager/Evangelist. I’d joined Microsoft Japan in 2003 and had been a Technical Architect in the public sector sales department, where he was responsible for raising awareness of .NET technology. In 2006, I had been transferred to the Developer Marketing Division, where I worked for 10 years as a technical evangelist in charge of technology education for iOS/Android and Microsoft Azure developers. After retiring from Microsoft Japan, I worked for Dell, Accenture, and others, and was appointed as an Executive Advisor to the Government CIO of the Cabinet Secretariat in April 2019. I have been leading the planning, development, and design of new government systems to realize the Declaration of the Most Advanced IT Nation from a cloud-by-default perspective. After serving as an evangelist at Cloud Venture in a dual role with an Executive Advisor to the Government CIO, I began working at Elastic in June 2020 and has been consistently involved in technology awareness activities and developer technology awareness for 17 years.
